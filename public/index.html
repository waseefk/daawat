Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that's loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, flavorful saffron, spices and herbs that is cooked over low heat for the most delectable flavors.

Mutton Biryani

A flavoursome, aromatic rice dish from the Middle East, with Indian style using long grain basmati rice, tender lamb marinated with rich spices and herbs, cooked over copper handi for exotic taste.

Hyderabadi Dum Biryani

Hyderabadi Dum Biryani is world famous dish from India, native to Hyderabad. This non-vegetarian delicacy is an authentic Hyderabadi rice preparation which is a meal in itself.

Veg Biryani

Authentic meal of fresh vegetables and rice marinated with richness of spices & yoghurt, infused with saffron and desi ghee, slowely cooked between layers of long grain basmati rice in handis.

Raitha

Mutton Gravy

Gulab Jamun

Phirni

Bread Halwa
